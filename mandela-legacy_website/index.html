Who We Are

At Mandela Legacy Gateway to Africa, our mission is to empower South African youth through comprehensive training and development, equipping them with the skills and knowledge to excel in the Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) industry.

We are driven by a commitment to impactful sourcing and to creating meaningful employment opportunities for South African youth.
