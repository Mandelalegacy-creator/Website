Vision Statement

Mission Statement

At Mandela Legacy Gateway to Africa, our mission is to empower South African youth through comprehensive training and development, equipping them with the skills and knowledge to excel in the Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) industry. We aim to provide top-tier BPO services to domestic and multinational organizations, with the added objective of driving innovation and excellence in emerging fields such as Generative AI and Data Engineering.

Our Values

At the heart of our values is a commitment to impactful sourcing. We are dedicated to creating employment opportunities for South African youth, fostering continuous training and education, and championing equity in the workplace.
